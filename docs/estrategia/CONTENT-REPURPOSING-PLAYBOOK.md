## METRICAS DE EXITO POR PIEZA

### Blog Post Original
- Trafico organico: 500+ visitas/mes (3 meses post-publicacion)
- Tiempo en pagina: >4 min
- Conversion a newsletter: >3%
- Conversion a lead magnet: >5%
- Backlinks: 2-3 minimo

### LinkedIn
- Thread: 5,000+ impresiones, 50+ reactions, 10+ comentarios
- Infografia: 3,000+ impresiones, 40+ reactions
- Video: 2,000+ views, 30+ reactions
- Carousel: 4,000+ impresiones, 60+ reactions
- Poll: 100+ votos, 15+ comentarios

### Newsletter
- Feature: 40%+ open, 10%+ click
- Nurture email: 35%+ open, 8%+ click, 1-2% booking

### Lead Magnet
- Descargas: 50+ primer mes
- Conversion a auditoria: 10-15%

### Social (Instagram/Twitter)
- Impresiones: 1,000+ por post
- Engagement rate: >3%
- Clicks a link: 20-30 por post
